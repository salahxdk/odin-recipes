Quiche

Description

Treat yourself to a slice of my savory quiche recipe. A buttery crust filled with a creamy egg custard, combined with your favorite ingredients like cheese, ham, spinach, and mushrooms, and baked to a golden perfection. Served hot or cold, it's the perfect dish for brunch, lunch, or dinner. Impress your guests with this versatile and flavorful recipe that's both easy and elegant.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups milk
  • 4 large eggs
  • ¾ cup biscuit baking mix
  • ¼ cup butter, softened
  • 1 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 (10 ounce) package chopped frozen broccoli, thawed and drained
  • 1 cup cubed cooked ham
  • 8 ounces shredded Cheddar cheese

Recipe Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C). Lightly grease a 10-inch quiche dish
  2. Beat milk, Parmesan cheese, baking mix, butter, and eggs in a large bowl until well combined; batter will be lumpy. Stir in broccoli, Cheddar cheese, and ham.
  3. Pour into the prepared quiche dish.
  4. Bake in the preheated oven for 50 minutes, until eggs are set and the top is golden brown.
  5. Enjoy
