French Toast

Description

"Indulge in a delicious breakfast with my French toast recipe. Thick slices of bread soaked in a rich custard mixture of eggs, milk, vanilla, and cinnamon, then cooked to golden perfection on a hot griddle. Serve it up with a generous drizzle of maple syrup and a dusting of powdered sugar for a breakfast treat that's both satisfying and comforting

Ingredients

  • ⅔ cup milk
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract (Optional)
  • ¼ teaspoon ground cinnamon (Optional)
  • Salt to taste
  • 6 thick slices of bread
  • 1 tablespoon unsalted butter, or more as needed

Recipe Steps

  1. Gather Ingredients
  2. Whisk Milk, Eggs, Vanilla, Cinnamon, and Salt together in a shallow bowl
  3. Lightly butter a griddle or skillet and heat over medium-high heat.
  4. Dunk bread in the egg mixture, soaking both sides.
  5. Transfer to the hot skillet and cook until golden, 3 to 4 minutes per side.
  6. Serve Hot
