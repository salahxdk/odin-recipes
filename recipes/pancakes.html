French Toast

Description

Start your day off right with my fluffy pancake recipe. Made with a simple batter of flour, sugar, baking powder, and eggs, these pancakes are light, airy, and oh-so-delicious. Cooked to a perfect golden brown on a hot griddle, they're sure to be a hit with the whole family. Top them with your favorite syrup, butter, and fresh fruit for a breakfast that's both tasty and satisfying.

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Sugar
  • Leaveners (Baking Powder and Salt)
  • Salt
  • Buttermilk
  • Milk
  • Eggs
  • Butter

Recipe Steps

  1. Mix all the Dry Ingredients
  2. Mix the Wet Ingredients together in a seperate bowl
  3. Lightly butter a griddle or skillet and heat over medium-high heat.
  4. Pour pancakes and cook on medium heat
  5. Cook until golden brown on each side
  6. Serve Hot
